# ClipLedger Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 22, 2026

ClipLedger is designed to keep clipboard history private and local to your Mac.

## Data Collection

ClipLedger does not collect personal data.

ClipLedger does not use analytics, tracking, advertising identifiers, crash reporting services, or third-party telemetry.

## Clipboard Data

ClipLedger monitors the macOS clipboard for plain text content and stores eligible clipboard entries locally on your Mac.

ClipLedger does not upload, sync, sell, share, or transmit clipboard contents to external servers.

ClipLedger ignores unsupported clipboard formats such as images, files, rich text, and HTML.

## Local Storage

Clipboard history, pinned items, tags, usage counts, and settings are stored locally on your Mac using Apple's local storage technologies.

You can delete individual clipboard entries or clear non-pinned clipboard history from within the app.

## Network Access

ClipLedger has no external server component and does not require an account.

The app does not contact external servers to operate.

## App Store Privacy

ClipLedger declares no collected data in its App Store privacy manifest.
